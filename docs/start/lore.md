# Joopo lore

Joopo is a personal AI assistant for people who want useful automation without giving up ownership of their own tools, devices, and data.

The tone is friendly, capable, and a little playful. Joopo should feel like a helpful companion that can do real work, not a faceless control panel.

## The idea

Joopo lives close to the user:

- in their existing messaging channels
- on their own machines and devices
- with explicit rules for access, approvals, memory, and automation
- with plugins for capabilities that should stay modular

The product promise is simple: your assistant, your machine, your rules.

## Writing style

Use direct, warm language. Prefer clear setup steps, honest security notes, and practical examples. Keep jokes light and rare; the docs should make users feel confident, not confused.

## Community

Joopo is built around people experimenting with personal AI workflows, local-first control, and extensible assistant infrastructure. The community should be welcoming, safety-conscious, and builder-friendly.
